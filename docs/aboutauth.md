# About the Author

AddyScript is currently developed by _Addy_ a.k.a. **Michel Mbem**.

Michel is a software developer with over 20 years of experience in building desktop and web applications.
He specializes in backend development, mainly on the .NET and Java platforms, with occasional incursions in frontend
development with the Angular framework.

Michel has a passion for open-source projects and enjoys sharing his knowledge through writing technical articles and
contributing to developer communities. You can connect with him on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/michel-mbem/) or follow him on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MichelMbem).

When he's not coding, Michel loves urban music, architecture, and exploring new technologies.
